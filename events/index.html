The DOM

Jargon
  • "Trees" as data structures
  • Each node may refer to other nodes, Children, which in turn may have their own children
  • Nested Structures
  • A node may NOT contain itself, directly, or indirectly.
  • Has single well defined root
  • In the DOM, document.documentElement serves as the root
  • Example of a child node would be document.body
  • Some can be leaf nodes, such as comments or pieces of text
